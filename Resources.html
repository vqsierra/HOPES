Crisis Support Resources

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988 
The 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline provides 24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress, and prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones.

Crisis Textline

Text HOME to 741-741
Crisis Text Line is here for any crisis. A live, trained Crisis Counselor receives the text and responds, all from our secure online platform. The volunteer Crisis Counselor will help you move from a hot moment to a cool moment.

The TREVOR Project

Call 1-866-488-7386
The Trevor Project is the leading national organization providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and questioning youth. These include a hotline and online, crisis intervention chat service.

Trans Lifeline

Call  1-877-565-8860
Trans Lifeline connects trans people to the community support and resources we need to survive and thrive.

Find A Helpline

Visit  findahelpline.com/
Global list of  helplines, hotlines & crisis lines. Get emotional support with suicidal thoughts, gender & sexual identity, depression, loneliness, and anxiety.
